Important Notes:

  • Press C to clear the screen
  • Press Esc to exit the program (Press Shift + Ctrl + Q to force close)
  • Press Q to bring up this window again
  • Press Shift + Z to restore the default or saved settings
  • Press Shift + Ctrl + V to open the Exception Window (Ctrl + C to clear log)
  • Press Ctrl + I to open the Stats Panel
  • Press Shift + X to open the Particle Graph Editor
  • Physics Mode only supports up to 1,000 collisions

Engine Controls:

  • Click or Click and Drag to create Particles
  • Press 0 to toggle Particle attraction to the mouse pointer
  • Press 1 to increase Particle size
  • Press 2 to decrease Particle size
  • Press 3 to decrease amount of Particles
  • Press 4 to advance the Molecule mode
  • Press 5 to toggle Reactive Colors
  • Press 6 to toggle Molecule Link mode
  • Press 7 to bring up the Options Menu
  • Press 8 to pause or un-pause the Engine
  • Press 9 to bring up the Particle Slide Editor
  • Press B to change the type of Link for Molecule Link mode
  • Press F to slow Particles
  • Press G to scatter Particles
  • Press M to toggle gravity for Physics mode
  • Press N to toggle debug info for Physics mode
  • Press R to change the background color
  • Press S to toggle graphics smoothing
  • Press T to toggle Particle friction
  • Press V to toggle connection type for Gravity Points
  • Press W to bring up the Reactive Colors Editor
  • Press Y to toggle Particle bounds checking
  • Press UP to increase Click and Drag amount
  • Press DOWN to decrease Click and Drag amount
  • Press Right to go to the next Molecule type
  • Press LEFT to go to the previous Molecule type
  • Press SPACE to create Gravity Points
